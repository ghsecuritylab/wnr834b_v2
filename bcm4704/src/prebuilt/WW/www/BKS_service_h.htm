Block Services Help

Block Services allows you to block Internet Access by specific users on your local network based on their IP address. For example, you can prevent the use of certain Internet services such as surfing the web (http) to everyone or just a few people on your network. Service Blocking can be configured to be on Never, on Per Schedule, or on Always.

To Enable Service Blocking:

  • Click Per Schedule to turn on Service Blocking and block according to the settings on the Schedule page.
  • Click Always to turn on Service Blocking to allow blocking all of the time, independent of the Schedule page.

To Disable Service Blocking:

  • Click Never to turn off Service Blocking.

Service Table

This table lists all Services which will currently be blocked. You can add and delete entries to this table as required.

To Add A New Service

  1. Click the Add button.
  2. Complete the resulting screen.
  3. Click the Apply button when finished.

To Edit A Service

  1. Select the radio button of the service you would like to edit.
  2. Click the Edit button.
  3. Change the data for the service as needed.
  4. Click the Accept button when finished.

To Delete A Service

  1. Click the radio button next to an row in the table.
  2. Click the Delete button to delete the selected service.

To Save Or Cancel Changes

Click Apply to save the new settings to the Router.
Click Cancel to discard any unsaved changes.
