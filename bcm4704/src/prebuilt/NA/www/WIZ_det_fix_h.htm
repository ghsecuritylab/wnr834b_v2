Smart Setup Wizard Help

Do you have a Fixed or Static IP Address Assigned by your ISP?

If you know that your ISP has provided a fixed IP address, click the "Yes" button, and the enter the IP address and related data from your ISP on the next screen.

If you are uncertain, click "Not sure". You will then see a setup screen which allows for either a fixed or dynamic IP address, and also contains a "Test" button. Refer to the data from your ISP to configure this screen.
